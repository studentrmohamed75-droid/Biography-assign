Ronaldo's Career

Cristiano Ronaldo (41 years old) is widely considered one of the greatest footballers in history, boasting over 970 official senior career goals for club and country, making him the top goalscorer of all time. Currently with Al-Nassr

Club Careers

Sporting CP (2002–2003): Began his professional career.

Manchester United (2003–2009; 2021–2022): Developed into a superstar, winning three Premier League titles and his first Champions League and Ballon d'Or.

Real Madrid (2009–2018): Became the club's all-time top scorer with 450 goals, winning four Champions League titles and four Ballon d'Or awards.

Juventus (2018–2021): Won two Serie A titles and scored over 100 goals.

Al-Nassr (2023–Present): Joined the Saudi Pro League side, becoming the first player to score 100 goals for four different top-flight clubs
