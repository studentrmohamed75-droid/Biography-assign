Cristiano Ronaldo

Who is Cristiano Ronaldo?

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro (born February 5, 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players in history. As of May 2026, the 41-year-old forward captains Saudi Pro League club Al-Nassr and the Portugal national team, holding records for the most international goals (143) and appearances (226)
